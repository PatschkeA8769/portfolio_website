About Body Grooming

It goes without saying we expect clients to visit us in a perfectly clean and hygienic condition. We do have shower facilites you are welcome to use if your appointment is after your day at work. As is common practice within the Health Industry, we use wherever possible and practical only disposable items. We strive for the highest standards in hygiene and cleanliness for your safety and comfort.

We offer clipping or shaving for men, and waxing or shaving for women. This includes eyebrow shaping (optional plucking), beard and moustache, back, chest, shoulders, arms, underarms, abdomen, legs, and speedo or bikini line.
