About Absolute Serenity

Welcome to Absolute Serenity. A small, professional therapy center that caters for the naturist client. Ladies, gentlemen and couples are all welcome by prior appointment only. We offer a Swedish massage and also grooming, based in a premises of the highest standard.

There are beautiful therapy rooms with sophisticated decor, whilst being warm, welcoming and very comfortable. Shower facilities are included. Outside are secure off-road parking for 4 vehicles on recorded CCTV camera, for your convenience and peace of mind.

Open Monday to Friday. Can offer some early evening appointments with enough notice. We're qualified and insured. It's best to call early mornings.
